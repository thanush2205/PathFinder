# PathFinder - User Guide for Visually Impaired Users

## 🎯 How PathFinder Helps You Navigate Safely

PathFinder is designed to be your **AI-powered eyes** that helps you navigate independently using just your voice and phone camera.

---

## 📱 Getting Started

### Initial Setup (One-time, with sighted helper)

1. **Install the App**
   - A helper installs PathFinder from Play Store/App Store
   - Opens the app and enables all permissions (Camera, Microphone, Location)

2. **Voice-Guided Registration**
   - App automatically starts voice guidance: *"Welcome to PathFinder. Let's set up your account."*
   - App asks: *"What is your name?"*
   - You say: "John Smith"
   - App asks: *"What is your email?"*
   - You say: "john@example.com"
   - App creates your account and says: *"Account created! You're all set."*

3. **Done!** From now on, everything is voice-controlled.

---

## 🗣️ Daily Use - How to Navigate

### Step 1: Wake Up the App

**Just say:** *"Hey PathFinder"* or *"PathFinder, help me"*

The app responds: *"Hello! I'm PathFinder, ready to assist you."*

---

### Step 2: Start Navigation

**You ask:** *"Help me navigate"* or *"Where can I go?"*

**PathFinder says:** 
- *"Starting navigation mode. Hold your phone at chest level, camera facing forward."*
- *"I'm now scanning your surroundings."*

---

### Step 3: Real-Time Environment Scanning

**What PathFinder Does Automatically:**

1. **Continuously scans the environment** using your phone's camera
2. **Detects objects** (people, furniture, doors, stairs, vehicles)
3. **Measures distances** to each object
4. **Announces obstacles** before you reach them

**Example Audio Feedback:**
- *"Person ahead, 3 meters"*
- *"Chair on your left, 1 meter - step right"*
- *"Door on your right, 2 meters"*
- *"Steps ahead, 5 meters - slow down"*
- *"Clear path ahead, you can continue walking"*

---

### Step 4: Ask Questions (Anytime)

You can ask PathFinder anything while navigating:

| **You Say** | **PathFinder Responds** |
|-------------|-------------------------|
| "Where am I?" | "You are at the main entrance of Building A, facing north." |
| "What's around me?" | "There's a bench 2 meters on your right, a person 4 meters ahead, and a door 3 meters on your left." |
| "Is there anyone nearby?" | "Yes, I detect 2 people - one ahead at 5 meters, another on your right at 3 meters." |
| "Find the exit" | "The exit is 10 meters ahead on your left. Walk straight, I'll guide you." |
| "Read the sign" | "The sign says: Emergency Exit This Way, with an arrow pointing right." |
| "Help me cross the street" | "Wait - I detect vehicles moving. I'll tell you when it's clear." |

## 🚶 Walking Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Walking in a Hallway

**PathFinder guides you:**

1. *"Clear path ahead, 5 meters"*
2. *"Wall on your right at 1.5 meters"* (helps you stay oriented)
3. *"Person approaching from ahead, 3 meters - slight right"*
4. *"Door ahead on left, 2 meters"*
5. *"You've reached the door"*

### Scenario 2: Navigating a Room

**You say:** "What's around me?"

**PathFinder responds:**
- *"You're in a room, approximately 4x5 meters"*
- *"Chair at 2 o'clock, 2 meters away"*
- *"Table at 10 o'clock, 3 meters"*
- *"Door behind you at 6 o'clock, 4 meters"*

### Scenario 3: Finding a Seat

**You say:** "Find me a chair"

**PathFinder:**
- *"I see 2 chairs. One on your left at 2 meters, another ahead at 4 meters."*
- *"Walk 3 steps forward and 2 steps left for the nearest chair."*
- *"You're right in front of the chair. Reach back with your hand."*

### Scenario 4: Reading Signs

**You say:** "Read what's ahead"

**PathFinder:**
- *"I see a sign at 3 meters ahead. It says: 'Cafeteria - Turn Right'"*

## 🎯 Key Features for Blind Users

### 1. **Hands-Free Operation**
- No need to touch the screen
- Everything works with voice
- Phone can be in your pocket (camera facing out) or held at chest level

### 2. **Real-Time Audio Alerts**
- Continuous environment monitoring
- Proactive obstacle warnings
- Distance and direction information

### 3. **Smart Object Recognition**
- Identifies: people, furniture, doors, stairs, vehicles, signs
- Distinguishes between static and moving objects
- Prioritizes important obstacles

### 4. **Text-to-Speech**
- Reads signs, labels, door numbers
- Announces locations
- Provides navigation instructions

### 5. **Screen Reader Compatible**
- Works with TalkBack (Android)
- Works with VoiceOver (iOS)
- All buttons have voice labels

---

## 📍 How Positioning Works

PathFinder uses:
- **Phone camera** - For visual object detection
- **GPS** - For outdoor location
- **Compass** - For direction (which way you're facing)
- **Motion sensors** - To detect when you're walking

You don't need to do anything - just hold the phone naturally and walk.

## 🆘 Emergency Features

### Get Help Quickly

**You say:** "Emergency" or "I need help"

**PathFinder:**
1. Captures your current location
2. Takes a photo of surroundings
3. Sends alert to your emergency contact
4. Provides your exact GPS coordinates
5. Says: *"Emergency alert sent to [contact name]"*

## 🔋 Battery & Data

- **Battery**: Uses camera and AI, plan for 3-4 hours of continuous use
- **Recommendation**: Use with portable battery pack
- **Data**: Works offline for basic navigation, online for advanced features

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Best Phone Position**: Hold at chest level, camera facing forward, slight downward angle
2. **Earphones**: Use for clearer audio, keeps your ears free to hear environment
3. **Practice**: Start in familiar places to learn the commands
4. **Feedback**: The more you use it, the better PathFinder learns your preferences
5. **Updates**: PathFinder improves with use and learns your common routes
